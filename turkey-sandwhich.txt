Ingredients:
 - 2 slices of turkey breast
 - 2 slices of bread
 - 1 slice provolone cheese
 - 1/4 cup mayonnaise

Directions:
  1. Place both slices of turkey breast to one slice of bread.
  2. Place the slice of provolone cheese on top of the turkey breast.
  3. Apply thin layer of mayonnaise onto other slice of bread.
  4. Combine both slices of bread so that the mayonnaise is directly
     on top of the provolone cheese.
